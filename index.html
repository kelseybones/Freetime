Social Life Organisation

Freetime is a mobile application that helps you get together with your friends and family more often by suggesting the dates and times that suit most people based on everyones calendars.

What is Freetime?

Freetime is an application that integrates with your calendars to suggest the best date to suit everyone for a get together, be it a birthday, holiday or just a quick catch up. Don't worry though, no one will be able to view your calendar apart from you, they will only be able to see if you're available that day or not.

Date Suggestions

We take the hard work out of getting together byt suggesting the date and time that suits most people based on their calendars. But don't worry, your privacy is the most important thing to us and no one will be able to see your calendar entries.

Calendar Integration

Freetime integrates with any and all calendars and updates automatically so your availability is always up to date. You can also edit you availability in app and block off periods of time you're always busy!

Create Events

Create events with as little or as much information as you want, all you need is an event name and off you go! We are completely flexible to what you know. Oh and by the way, there's no cap on how many events you can make!

Create Groups

Create groups with just you and someone else or with your whole family. We don't belive in putting caps on our groups so you can add as many or a little people as you want!
