How did Freetime start?

Freetime was born to fix a real life problem. Founders Kelsey and Jordan hated trying to organise getting together with their friends, especially when everyone starting moving to different countries for work. It often took months of planning and there was always one person who couldn't make it. It became a personal challenge to find a way to make this small life problem easier.

Who is Freetime?

Freetime is (for now) a small team of just two. Kelsey and Jordan are partners in crime and partners in business. Kelsey is a UX designer, Jordan is an iOS developer. They like to call themselves a power couple ( ðŸ¤¢ ) but truthfully their skill sets were perfect for developing Freetime.
